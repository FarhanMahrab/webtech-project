Hotel Policies

To ensure a comfortable and safe stay for all our guests, please review the following policies.

Check-In & Check-Out

  • Check-in time: 3:00 PM
  • Check-out time: 12:00 PM (noon)
  • Early check-in or late check-out is subject to availability

Reservations & Payments

  • All bookings require valid guest information
  • Payments can be made online or at reception
  • Rates may vary depending on season and availability

Children & Extra Beds

  • Children are welcome
  • Extra beds may be available upon request
  • Additional charges may apply

Pets

  • Pets are allowed inside guest rooms

Safety & Conduct

  • Guests must follow hotel safety guidelines
  • Smoking is prohibited inside rooms
  • Damage to hotel property will incur charges

Cancellations

  • Cancellations should be made at least 24 hours before check-in
  • Late cancellations may result in charges
